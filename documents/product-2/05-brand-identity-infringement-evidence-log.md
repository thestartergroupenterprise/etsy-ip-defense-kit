## HOW TO USE THIS DOCUMENT

Open a new copy of this log for every infringement incident. Fill it in the moment you discover the infringement, memory fades and timestamps matter. Courts, platform moderators, and attorneys all look for a complete, contemporaneous record. A well-documented case gets resolved faster. An undocumented case drags on.
