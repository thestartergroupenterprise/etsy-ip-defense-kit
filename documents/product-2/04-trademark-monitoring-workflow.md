**TRADEMARK PROTECTION RESOURCE TOOLKIT, Document 4 of 6**
*Brand Protection Tools for Independent Sellers and Small Creative Businesses*

---

## HOW TO USE THIS DOCUMENT

Run the weekly sweep below every 7 days. The full sweep takes 20 to 30 minutes. The monthly deep scan adds another 30 to 45 minutes. Catching infringement early, before a copycat builds SEO authority or sales history under your brand name, dramatically reduces the damage and the cost of enforcement. The sellers who recover fastest are the ones who found out first.

### WHEN YOU FIND SOMETHING

**Immediate actions (do these before contacting anyone):**
1. Screenshot the infringing content, full page, URL visible, with timestamp
2. Save the URL, copy the exact listing or profile URL
3. Record seller information, name, location if visible, account age if visible
4. Do not contact the infringing seller directly without reading Document 5 first

**Triage, how serious is it?**

| Severity | Description | Action |
|----------|-------------|--------|
| HIGH | Someone is using your exact brand name as their business name or registered a domain | File platform report immediately using Document 3, consult attorney if USPTO filing involved |
| MEDIUM | Someone is using your brand name in product titles to appear in searches for your brand | File platform trademark report using Document 3 |
| MEDIUM | Someone is using your logo or visual brand elements | This may be copyright infringement (see the IP Defense Kit DMCA toolkit) OR trademark violation, file both if applicable |
| LOW | New competitor with a similar name but clearly different branding | Monitor closely, document in this log, no immediate action required |
