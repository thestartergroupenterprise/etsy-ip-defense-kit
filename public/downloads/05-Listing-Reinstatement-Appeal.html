Listing Reinstatement Appeal Template

ETSY IP DEFENSE KIT — Document 5 of 5 Professional IP Protection Templates for Etsy Sellers

HOW TO USE THIS TEMPLATE

This template is for a specific and infuriating scenario: a thief steals your photos, someone reports THEIR listing to Etsy using YOUR images as "evidence," and Etsy's automated system suspends YOUR listing — the original. Or: Etsy removes your listing after a copyright complaint you believe is fraudulent or made in error. Fill in every [BRACKETED] field. This appeal is sent via Etsy's case system or via email to copyright@etsy.com. Response time varies (3–10 business days). Stay factual, calm, and evidence-heavy. Emotional appeals have lower success rates than evidence-based ones.

LISTING REINSTATEMENT APPEAL

DATE: [DATE — e.g., March 20, 2026]

TO: Etsy Trust & Safety / Copyright Team EMAIL: copyright@etsy.com SUBJECT: Listing Reinstatement Request — [YOUR SHOP NAME] — Listing #[LISTING ID] — [YOUR CASE NUMBER IF KNOWN]

FROM: [YOUR FULL LEGAL NAME] [YOUR ETSY SHOP NAME] [YOUR ETSY SHOP URL] [YOUR REGISTERED EMAIL ADDRESS on Etsy]

Dear Etsy Trust & Safety Team,

I am writing to appeal the removal of my Etsy listing and to request its immediate reinstatement.

THE LISTING IN QUESTION

Listing title: [YOUR LISTING TITLE — e.g., "Handmade Sterling Silver Moon Ring — Oxidized Finish"] Listing URL: [YOUR LISTING URL — e.g., https://www.etsy.com/listing/123456789/your-listing-title] Listing ID: [LISTING NUMBER — found in your Shop Manager → Listings] Date removed: [DATE YOUR LISTING WAS TAKEN DOWN] Reason given by Etsy: [COPY THE EXACT REASON FROM ETSY'S EMAIL — e.g., "Reported for copyright infringement" or "Removed following a DMCA notice"]

WHY THIS REMOVAL IS IN ERROR

[Choose the section that applies to your situation. Delete the others.]

OPTION A — Thief's listing was reported, yours was caught in error:

I am the original creator of the photographs and content in this listing. My listing at [YOUR LISTING URL] is the ORIGINAL source of the images. The infringing party is/was [INFRINGING SHOP NAME], whose listing at [INFRINGING LISTING URL] used my original photographs without permission.

It appears Etsy's review process may have confused the original and infringing listings. I am the copyright owner of these images, not the infringer.

OPTION B — You received a DMCA takedown from someone you believe filed fraudulently:

I received notification that a DMCA takedown notice was filed against my listing by [NAME OF PERSON/ENTITY IF KNOWN OR "an unknown party"]. I believe this takedown was filed in error or in bad faith because:

[EXPLAIN YOUR REASON — e.g., "I am the original creator of all content in this listing, including the photographs and written description. I have documentation showing I created this content on [DATE], prior to any competing listing." OR "I have not given any party the right to claim copyright ownership over my original creative work."]

OPTION C — Your listing was removed following a competitor's false report:

My listing was removed following what I believe is a false or retaliatory copyright complaint. The listing in question contains:

  • Original photographs taken by me / on my behalf
  • Original written descriptions authored by me
  • My own original product design

    • No other party holds copyright over the content in my listing.

    PROOF OF ORIGINAL OWNERSHIP

    I am providing the following evidence to establish that I am the original copyright owner:

    Evidence Item 1 — Original Creation Date: [DESCRIBE — e.g., "The original photograph file, attached, contains EXIF metadata showing it was created on [DATE EARLIER THAN THEIRS]. This is earlier than any competing listing's creation date." OR "My original Etsy listing was published on [DATE] — screenshot attached — which predates the infringing listing at [INFRINGING URL] which was published on [THEIR DATE]."]

    Evidence Item 2 — Original File or Creation Proof: [DESCRIBE — e.g., "Attached: the original unedited RAW/JPEG photo file from my camera, taken [DATE]. The file name, metadata, and creation timestamp confirm my ownership." OR "Attached: screenshot of my original Etsy listing showing publication date of [DATE], which predates any competing listing."]

    Evidence Item 3 — Additional Proof (if available): [DESCRIBE — e.g., "Attached: screenshots from my Instagram account (@[YOUR HANDLE]) showing I posted photos of this product on [DATE EARLIER THAN THEIRS]." OR "Attached: invoice from the photographer I commissioned, dated [DATE]." OR "Attached: screenshots showing my social media posts featuring these photos prior to any infringing listing." OR delete this section if you have only 2 items.]

    REQUEST

    Based on the evidence above, I request:

    • Immediate reinstatement of my listing at [LISTING URL]
    • Review and removal of the infringing listing at [INFRINGING LISTING URL], if it has not already been removed
    • Written confirmation that my account is in good standing and that this removal will not be counted against my shop's record

    DMCA COUNTER-NOTICE (if you received a DMCA takedown — include this section if applicable)

    Include this section only if Etsy removed your listing specifically due to a DMCA notice from a third party. Delete if not applicable.

    Pursuant to 17 U.S.C. § 512(g), I am filing a formal counter-notification to the DMCA takedown received on [DATE OF TAKEDOWN NOTICE].

    I hereby state under penalty of perjury that:

    • I have a good faith belief that the material was removed or disabled as a result of mistake or misidentification of the material to be removed or disabled.
    • I am the original creator and copyright owner of the material at issue. The material removed from my listing is my original work, created by me on or around [DATE OF CREATION].
    • I consent to the jurisdiction of the Federal District Court for the [YOUR JUDICIAL DISTRICT — e.g., "Northern District of California" if you don't know yours, use the district where Etsy is headquartered: Eastern District of New York], and I will accept service of process from the person who provided the notification or an agent of such person.

    Signature: [YOUR FULL LEGAL NAME — typed or handwritten if printing] Name: [YOUR FULL LEGAL NAME] Date: [DATE] Address: [YOUR MAILING ADDRESS — required for valid counter-notice] Email: [YOUR EMAIL ADDRESS]

    ATTACHMENTS

    Please find the following attachments to this appeal:

    • ☐ Screenshot of my original listing with URL and publication date visible
    • ☐ Screenshot of the infringing listing (if applicable)
    • ☐ Original image file(s) with EXIF metadata (creation date)
    • ☐ [ANY ADDITIONAL EVIDENCE — social media posts, invoices, etc.]

    CLOSING

    I take the accuracy of this appeal seriously and submit it in good faith. All statements above are true to the best of my knowledge. I am available to provide any additional documentation needed to resolve this matter.

    Please confirm receipt of this appeal and advise on the expected review timeline.

    Thank you,

    [YOUR FULL NAME] [YOUR ETSY SHOP NAME] [YOUR EMAIL ADDRESS] [DATE]

    SENDING THIS APPEAL

    Primary method:

  • Email to: copyright@etsy.com
  • Subject line: Listing Reinstatement Request — [SHOP NAME] — Listing #[ID]
  • Attach all evidence items
  • Send and screenshot the sent email

    • Secondary method (if you have a case number):

  • Log in to Etsy Help Center
  • Go to your open case
  • Reply to the case with this appeal text + attachments

    • After sending:

  • Set a 3-day reminder to follow up if no response
  • If no response after 7 days: resend with "FOLLOW-UP — Second Request" added to the subject line

    • FOLLOW-UP EMAIL TEMPLATE

    Use this if you don't receive a response within 7 business days:

    Subject: FOLLOW-UP — Listing Reinstatement Request — [SHOP NAME] — Listing #[ID] — [ORIGINAL DATE FILED]

    Dear Etsy Trust & Safety Team,
    >
    I submitted a listing reinstatement appeal on [DATE OF ORIGINAL APPEAL] for listing #[LISTING ID] ([LISTING TITLE]). I have not received a response or resolution as of [TODAY'S DATE].
    >
    My listing has now been suspended for [NUMBER] days, resulting in ongoing lost revenue. I am requesting urgent review.
    >
    I am reattaching my original appeal and all supporting evidence. Please advise on the current status.
    >
    [YOUR NAME]
    [YOUR SHOP NAME]

    ESCALATION IF APPEAL IS DENIED

    If Etsy denies your appeal and you believe the denial is in error:

    • File again with additional evidence (stronger is better — add more proof of your original creation date)
    • Post in the Etsy Seller Community (community.etsy.com) — document your situation factually. Other sellers and Etsy staff read these forums.
    • Contact Etsy via social media (Twitter/X: @etsy or @EtsySells) — public visibility sometimes accelerates response
    • Consult an IP attorney — if your shop generates significant revenue, a formal legal letter from an attorney often produces faster results than seller appeals
    • File with the Better Business Bureau — Etsy monitors BBB complaints and often responds to them
