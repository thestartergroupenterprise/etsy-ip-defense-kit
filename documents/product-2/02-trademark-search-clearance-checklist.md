## HOW TO USE THIS DOCUMENT

Complete every search before using a new brand name commercially, before expanding to a new platform, or before filing a trademark application. A missed conflict can cost you everything you've built under a brand. This checklist takes 30 to 90 minutes. A trademark attorney charges $300 to $600 per hour for this work. Do it yourself first, then retain counsel only if something concerning shows up.
