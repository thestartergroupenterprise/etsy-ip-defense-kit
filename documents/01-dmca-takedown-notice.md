## HOW TO USE THIS TEMPLATE

Fill in every field marked with [BRACKETS]. Remove all bracketed placeholders before sending, no brackets should appear in your final notice. This template is calibrated for Etsy's live IP reporting portal (not the broken Help Center link), and is also valid for Temu, AliExpress, and general web use. Send one notice per infringing listing.

## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC FILING INSTRUCTIONS

### ETSY (Primary Method, Use the IP Portal, NOT Help Center)

**The correct URL:** https://www.etsy.com/legal/ip/

1. Go to: https://www.etsy.com/legal/ip/
2. Select "Copyright Infringement"
3. You do NOT need to paste this full letter, Etsy's form will prompt you for each field
4. Use this document as your source of truth for every answer
5. In the "Description" field, use the language from the IDENTIFICATION OF INFRINGING MATERIAL section above
6. Submit. You will receive a case number by email within 24 - 48 hours.
7. If you don't receive a case number, the form did not submit correctly. Try again or email copyright@etsy.com with this full notice.

**Etsy's DMCA Agent (for email submissions):**
Etsy, Inc.
117 Adams Street
Brooklyn, NY 11201
Email: copyright@etsy.com

---

### TEMU

**Temu IP Reporting Portal:** https://www.temu.com/ip-protect.html

1. Visit the portal above
2. Select "Copyright"
3. Fill in each field using this document as your source
4. Upload screenshots of both your original and their infringing listing side-by-side
5. Temu typically responds within 3 - 5 business days

**Temu IP Email (backup):** ip@temu.com

---

### ALIEXPRESS

**AliExpress IP Protection Platform:** https://ipp.alibabagroup.com/

1. Create a free account at the portal above (required, you cannot file without an account)
2. Select "Copyright" → "Create Complaint"
3. Upload proof of ownership (your original file with metadata, or your Etsy listing with original publication date)
4. Paste infringing URLs into the provided field
5. AliExpress typically responds within 5 - 7 business days

**AliExpress IP Email (backup):** aliexpress.ip@alibaba-inc.com

---

### GENERAL WEB (hosting providers, other platforms)

**Find the DMCA Agent:**
1. Go to https://dmca.copyright.gov/osp/search.html
2. Search for the platform name to find their registered DMCA agent
3. Send this full notice to the DMCA agent's listed contact information

**Or use:** The platform's standard "Report Content" or "Report Abuse" flow, referencing this notice in the description field.
