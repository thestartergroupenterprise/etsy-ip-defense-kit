DMCA Takedown Notice Template

ETSY IP DEFENSE KIT — Document 1 of 5 Professional IP Protection Templates for Etsy Sellers

HOW TO USE THIS TEMPLATE

Fill in every field marked with [BRACKETS]. Remove all bracketed placeholders before sending — no brackets should appear in your final notice. This template is calibrated for Etsy's live IP reporting portal (not the broken Help Center link), and is also valid for Temu, AliExpress, and general web use. Send one notice per infringing listing.

DMCA TAKEDOWN NOTICE

Date: [DATE — e.g., March 20, 2026]

To: [PLATFORM DMCA AGENT — see platform-specific instructions below]

From: [YOUR FULL LEGAL NAME] [YOUR BUSINESS NAME, if applicable] [YOUR MAILING ADDRESS] [YOUR EMAIL ADDRESS] [YOUR PHONE NUMBER, optional]

IDENTIFICATION OF COPYRIGHTED WORK

I am the owner (or authorized agent of the owner) of the following copyrighted work(s):

Type of work: [DESCRIBE YOUR WORK — e.g., original product photography, original graphic design, original written listing description, original jewelry design rendered in images, etc.]

Title/Description: [DESCRIBE YOUR SPECIFIC WORK — e.g., "My original product photos showing a handmade sterling silver moon ring on white background, taken by me on [DATE YOU CREATED IT]"]

Location of original work:

  • My Etsy shop: [YOUR ETSY SHOP URL — e.g., https://www.etsy.com/shop/YourShopName]
  • Original listing: [YOUR ORIGINAL LISTING URL]
  • [OPTIONAL: Additional location where original is hosted, e.g., your website or portfolio]

    • Date of creation: [DATE YOU FIRST CREATED THIS WORK — approximate is fine]

    IDENTIFICATION OF INFRINGING MATERIAL

    The following material infringes my copyright and must be removed immediately:

    Infringing listing/URL #1: [FULL URL OF INFRINGING LISTING]

    Description of infringement: [DESCRIBE HOW IT INFRINGES — e.g., "This listing uses my original product photographs without authorization. The images are identical/substantially similar to my original photos and were copied directly from my Etsy listing."]

    Infringing listing/URL #2 (if applicable): [FULL URL OF INFRINGING LISTING — delete this block if not needed]

    Description of infringement: [DESCRIBE HOW IT INFRINGES]

    (Add additional listings by copying the block above. Submit separate notices if filing against multiple sellers.)

    STATEMENT OF GOOD FAITH

    I have a good faith belief that the use of the copyrighted material described above is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.

    STATEMENT OF ACCURACY

    I swear, under penalty of perjury, that the information in this notification is accurate and that I am the copyright owner or am authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner.

    SIGNATURE

    Signature: [YOUR HANDWRITTEN OR ELECTRONIC SIGNATURE] Printed Name: [YOUR FULL LEGAL NAME] Title: [Owner / Authorized Agent — choose one] Date: [DATE]

    PLATFORM-SPECIFIC FILING INSTRUCTIONS

    ETSY (Primary Method — Use the IP Portal, NOT Help Center)

    The correct URL: https://www.etsy.com/legal/ip/

    • Go to: https://www.etsy.com/legal/ip/
    • Select "Copyright Infringement"
    • You do NOT need to paste this full letter — Etsy's form will prompt you for each field
    • Use this document as your source of truth for every answer
    • In the "Description" field, use the language from the IDENTIFICATION OF INFRINGING MATERIAL section above
    • Submit. You will receive a case number by email within 24–48 hours.
    • If you don't receive a case number, the form did not submit correctly. Try again or email copyright@etsy.com with this full notice.

    Etsy's DMCA Agent (for email submissions): Etsy, Inc. 117 Adams Street Brooklyn, NY 11201 Email: copyright@etsy.com

    TEMU

    Temu IP Reporting Portal: https://www.temu.com/ip-protect.html

    • Visit the portal above
    • Select "Copyright"
    • Fill in each field using this document as your source
    • Upload screenshots of both your original and their infringing listing side-by-side
    • Temu typically responds within 3–5 business days

    Temu IP Email (backup): ip@temu.com

    ALIEXPRESS

    AliExpress IP Protection Platform: https://ipp.alibabagroup.com/

    • Create a free account at the portal above (required — you cannot file without an account)
    • Select "Copyright" → "Create Complaint"
    • Upload proof of ownership (your original file with metadata, or your Etsy listing with original publication date)
    • Paste infringing URLs into the provided field
    • AliExpress typically responds within 5–7 business days

    AliExpress IP Email (backup): aliexpress.ip@alibaba-inc.com

    GENERAL WEB (hosting providers, other platforms)

    Find the DMCA Agent:

  • Go to https://dmca.copyright.gov/osp/search.html
  • Search for the platform name to find their registered DMCA agent
  • Send this full notice to the DMCA agent's listed contact information

    • Or use: The platform's standard "Report Content" or "Report Abuse" flow, referencing this notice in the description field.

    RECORD-KEEPING

    After filing, immediately log the following in your monitoring tracker:

    • Date filed: [DATE]
    • Platform: [PLATFORM]
    • Case/Reference number: [NUMBER FROM CONFIRMATION EMAIL]
    • Infringing URL(s): [URLS]
    • Status: Filed → Pending → [Resolved / Rejected / Escalated]
    • Follow-up date: [DATE + 7 DAYS]
